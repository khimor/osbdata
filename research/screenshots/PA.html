The PA Gaming Control Board is dedicated to ensuring the public is informed about the gross revenue and tax revenue generated across all gaming markets regulated by the Board. Revenue reports are available in both PDF and Excel formats and can be accessed by selecting a state fiscal year and the desired type of gaming.
